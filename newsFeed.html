Public Release of NSS President's Personal E-mail

On March 12, 2018 personal e-mails were released to the public on a website operated by Jacob Henderson. Mr. Henderson reportedly attended NSS (Nashville Software School), located in Nashville, TN, many years ago. It is not clear what the motive would be if he actually is responsible for hacking into the e-mail system. Some speculate he was still angry over the "Coffee Pot" incedent of 2017. While no one has come forward to say for sure Henderson is responsible, the Metro Police Department is investigating his likely involvement.

Likelihood of Henderson's Involvement 9/10



"DonkeyMan" Tried to Profit From HCA Security System Defect

On May 1, 2018, a grey hat hacker reached out to HCA to notify them of a sercurity breach at which point they offered to fix the error for payment through CryptoCurrency. After the Metro Police Department in Nashville investigated the interaction, they found that many people who knew him, tied Henderson to the crime due to his love of CryptoCurrency and the band DonkeyMan. As Henderson has been on the lamb for months, police speculate he is quickly running out of funds.
